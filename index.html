Flight Path Network Diagram

This chart visualises flight routes between different cities. From this, we see that airports with the most flights are located around the Europe and North

Flight Duration vs Average Ticket Price Scatterplot

This scatterplot shows the relationship between the flight duration and the average ticket price. It is expected that the longer the flight, the more expensive the ticket. However, it seems that there are quite a few flights with minimal flight durations that cost more than flights with long hours.

Flight Delays and Cancellations per Hour of Day

This chart breaks down the counts of flight delays and cancellations depending on the hour of the day. This helps in understanding common time periods where disruptions are more common (such as peak travel times).
